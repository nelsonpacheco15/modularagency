About the studio

Built between two worlds, on purpose.

Modular started in Taipei in 2020 because we kept seeing brilliant Taiwanese companies struggle to be understood abroad — and Western agencies struggle to understand Taiwan. We're the studio that sits in the middle.

A small studio with an unusual centre of gravity.

We're foreigners who chose Taiwan, and Taiwanese who've spent careers abroad. That mix matters — it's what lets us write English copy that doesn't sound translated, and read a factory floor that an outsider would miss.

Our clients are the companies that built modern Taiwan and want to be known by the world they already supply: ebike makers, hardware brands, manufacturers, software teams. We help them say what they actually do, in language the rest of the world recognises.
